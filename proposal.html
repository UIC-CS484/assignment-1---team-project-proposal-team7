RESTful API

We are planning to use Google Maps RESTful API. It will give users the ability to rate a place and save/bookmark it in their profile. The places they rate will show up on their map and to the public only if they choose to. We will collect most popular restaurants who rate by user, email/number, password, places that they review, and Location history ONLY IF they opt in.

User type and Goal

People who want to travel to new places and check out what's good beforehand. Also who are interested in other people's suggestions or suggestions from people out of their immediate circle.

Goal:

- Provide an easy way to recommend shop/restaurants etc.

- Then use intuitive visual cues like a heat map on google maps to show places that are "hot" and that are not.

- User can choose to share their suggestions with people close them (location wise)
